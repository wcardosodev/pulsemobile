How can I send messages from this app?

1a) Go to the "Availability" page, you can do this from the navigation bar at the top by tapping the icon.

1b) Or by opening the side navigation bar using the icon and tapping "Availability".

2) Once you are on the availability, you will need to find the month you are inputting your availabilty for. The calendar will default to the current month, you can tap the and icons next to the Month/Year Header to go back or forward a month.

3) When you have the correct month selected, you can then pick the date you wish from the calendar by tapping on it.

4) When you have selected the date you wish to add to, it will open up an overlay. You will be able to see the date you are inputting your availability for at the top and you can select, using the radio buttons, what you wish to input for that day. For example, if you are Available for an Early or Late shift, but Not Available for a Night shift you can tick the radio buttons as shown below.

5) When you are happy, tap the "Submit Availability" button to submit your availability.
